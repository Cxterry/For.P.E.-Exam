# 1 在...附近
1. 在...附近，范围比较模糊
she's standing by the tree over there.     
精确范围：in front of ,behind
2. stand by sb
支持某人.
3. 引申：短暂停留
she needed to go by the bank .
I'll come by this evening .

# 2 表示方式方法
+抽象的方式
by car/by bus/train

# 3 表示变化的程度
by +数量词 可以翻译为了
They lead by two second
The bullet missed him by two inches
Student complaints are rising by 10% a year.
A decent winner always tries to beat the opponent by no more than one or two points as a gesture of respect for the other side. 以...之差

# 4 表示no after
Can you finish the work by five o'clock?
The report must be finished by tomorrow

# 5 表示经过
可以用作介词，也可以用作名词
用作介词时后面一定加名词，构成介词短语
go by the station
as time goes by by去修饰go
He walked by me without speaking
